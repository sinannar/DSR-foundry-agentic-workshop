# Step 11: Publish an agent (optional)

## Objectives

- Understand when and why agents are published.
- Publish the `retail-assistant` agent and confirm its published state.

## Steps

1. Open the `retail-assistant` agent in the portal.
1. Review the publishing options and what publishing makes available to
   consumers.
1. Publish the agent:
   1. Confirm the model, tools, and knowledge are configured as intended.
   1. Complete the publish action.
1. Confirm the agent shows a published state.
1. Note how a published agent can be consumed and where to find its connection
   details.

## Validation

- The agent shows a published state after the publish action.
- You can locate the published agent's connection or consumption details.

## Troubleshooting

- If publishing is unavailable, confirm your role grants permission to publish
  in the project.
- If publishing fails, confirm the agent configuration is complete and saved.
- If consumers cannot reach the agent, confirm the published connection details
  and required access.
